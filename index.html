Graph-Based Legal IR

Evidence-first legal information retrieval for Chilean law.

We started with focused domains — environmental and labor law — to validate the methodology before expanding. This repository documents the architecture, methodology, and current status.

What Makes This Different

  • Evidence-first: relations grounded in exact text, not paraphrase
  • Fail-closed: abstains when evidence is insufficient
  • Human review: ambiguous edges require validation
  • Incremental domains: prove on small scopes first

Current Domains

  • Environmental law — pilot domain, ingestion complete
  • Labor law — expansion domain, ~4,000 QA pairs, scrapers operational
  • Citizen-facing — planned (Lexito)

Domain strategy: prove on small scopes before expanding.

Current Status

Retrieval operational (IndexO + BETO). LexO-Alpha agent active via Telegram. Procurador-digital MVP for labor deadlines. Core repos private during consolidation.
