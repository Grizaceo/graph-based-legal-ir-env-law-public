# Public Roadmap

## Current Phase

- Consolidate MVP architecture and evidence controls.
- Stabilize review workflows and baseline evaluation quality.
- Maintain strict separation between reviewed and non-reviewed material.

## Next Milestone

- Prepare a constrained public demo surface.
- Expose inspectable legal graph views and evidence-backed examples.
- Publish clearer public-facing usage boundaries and limitations.

## Later Phase

- Evolve toward timeline/state/transition logic for richer legal modeling.
- Introduce LGWM-oriented components progressively.
- Keep release gates aligned with evidence quality and review maturity.

This roadmap describes direction and sequencing, not a claim that all later-phase capabilities are already in production.
